Item #: CTR-001 - Street Cat

Object Class: Opasni

Special Containment Procedures: CTR-001 is to be monitored at all times when spotted near the ████████ park.

Description: CTR-001 appears to be a stray feline of unknown origin. It is commonly seen near public spaces and appears friendly, though its behavior is anomalous when exposed to small rodents.

Item #: CTR-002 - Domestic Dog

Object Class: Bezopasni

Special Containment Procedures: CTR-002 is to be contained in a standard domestic environment with access to food, water, and exercise.

Description: CTR-002 is a domesticated canine with no observable anomalous properties. Reports have suggested a heightened intelligence.
